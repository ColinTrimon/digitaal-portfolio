My interests

I am fascinated with culture and history. I know a lot about the colonisation of the Americas, the dark ages, Russian history, the world wars and contemporary history (1945 to present). I am also interested in politics, but I only discuss politics with my closest friends and family. I'll refuse to talk with anyone else, no matter how trivial the topic might be.

I always have trouble explaining my passion for culture, I have always been interested in it. Especially language, other than my native Dutch I speak English (duh) and I am studying Russian. I can read and write, but I am far from fluent. I can listen to a conversation in Russian and usually make out what they are saying but speaking is still beyond me. If I become fluent in Russian I would be interested in learning Chinese, it seems like an interesting language and the difficulty does not scare me. It actually encourages me, I would feel very proud if I could speak it. If I will not learn Chinese, I would probably learn Spanish, Portuguese, Italian or Greek.
